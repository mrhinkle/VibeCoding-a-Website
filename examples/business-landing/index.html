Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a typical engagement take?

Most clients see initial results within 3-6 months. Strategy engagements are typically 3 months, implementation projects 6-12 months depending on complexity. Enterprise transformations can span 12+ months with multiple initiatives.

Do you work with companies new to AI?

Absolutely. Many of our clients are starting their AI journey. We begin with strategy and education, ensuring your team understands the concepts before we build. Our training and support make the transition smooth.

What's your approach to data security?

Data security is paramount. We follow industry best practices, comply with regulations like GDPR and HIPAA, and implement proper access controls. All data remains your property, and we use encryption for sensitive information.

Can you work with our existing systems?

Yes. We assess your current infrastructure and build solutions that integrate seamlessly. Whether you use cloud platforms, on-premises systems, or a hybrid approach, we adapt to your environment.

What if the project scope changes?

We build flexibility into our engagements. If opportunities emerge or priorities shift, we adjust the roadmap and timeline accordingly. Your needs drive our approach.

How do you measure success?

We establish clear metrics at the start: business impact (revenue, cost savings), operational metrics (efficiency, quality), and technical metrics (model accuracy, system performance). We track progress throughout and report regularly.
