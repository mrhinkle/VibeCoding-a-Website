The S.M.A.R.T. Framework

> A 5-step process for AI workflow automation

S
Sort
Audit your workflows to find high-value targets. Don't automate everything.
M
Match
Align tasks with the right AI capabilities (LLM vs. traditional software).
A
Automate
Build the "lazy" first draft system. Connect the pipes.
R
Refine
Add the human loop. Polish the output. Improve the prompt.
T
Take Control
Scale the system. Document it. Delegate it.
