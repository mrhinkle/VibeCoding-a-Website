# VibeCoding Template Gallery

Welcome to the VibeCoding template gallery! These pre-configured templates give you a head start on building your website without starting from a blank page.

## How to Use Templates

Tell Claude which template you want to use. Simply say:

- "I want the consultant template"
- "Start from the fitness studio template"
- "Show me the nonprofit template"
- "Let's use the freelancer portfolio template"

Claude will load the template's configuration and help you build your site with pre-filled content, colors, fonts, and page structure tailored to your business type.

## Available Templates

| Template | Target Audience | Business Type | Key Pages |
|----------|-----------------|---------------|-----------|
| **Consultant** | Solo business consultants (strategy, management) | Professional services | Home, About, Services, Case Studies, Contact |
| **Fitness Studio** | Yoga studios, gyms, fitness centers | Wellness & fitness | Home, Classes/Schedule, Instructors, Pricing, Contact |
| **Restaurant** | Neighborhood restaurants, cafes, bistros | Food & beverage | Home, Menu, About/Story, Reservations, Contact |
| **Nonprofit** | Community nonprofits, charities, NGOs | Nonprofit & mission-driven | Home, Mission, Programs, Get Involved/Donate, News, Contact |
| **Freelancer** | Designers, developers, writers, creatives | Portfolio & services | Home, Portfolio, About, Services, Contact |

## What's Included in Each Template

Each template directory contains:

- **site-brief.json** — Pre-filled business information and site structure
- **brand-config.json** — Color palette, typography, and tone guidelines
- **description.md** — Overview of the template and what you'll build

Pick the template that best matches your business, and let Claude guide you through the VibeCoding 19-skill system to bring your vision to life!
