# Fitness Studio Template

**For yoga studios, gyms, and fitness centers offering classes and wellness programs.**

This vibrant template is perfect for fitness studios looking to attract new members and showcase their community atmosphere. The energetic coral and teal color scheme creates an inviting, dynamic feel that speaks to health and vitality.

## Visual Style

The fitness studio template features bright, action-oriented imagery of classes in progress, happy members, and the studio space. The design uses energetic coral (#ff6b6b) as the primary color paired with refreshing teal (#20c997) accents. Modern sans-serif fonts convey accessibility and energy.

## Pages Included

- **Home** — Hero section with dynamic studio images, featured classes, and prominent CTA for free trial classes
- **Classes/Schedule** — Complete weekly schedule with class types, times, duration, difficulty levels, and instructor names
- **Instructors** — Individual instructor profiles with photos, bios, specialties, certifications, and class schedule
- **Pricing** — Clear membership tiers (monthly, quarterly, annual), class packages, drop-in rates, and special trial offers
- **Contact** — Contact form, studio location with map, business hours, phone number, and direct class booking

This template emphasizes community, transformation, and accessibility to motivate new members to join your studio.
