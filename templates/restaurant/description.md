# Restaurant Template

**For neighborhood restaurants, cafes, and bistros serving diners and families.**

This elegant template is designed for restaurants looking to showcase their cuisine, ambiance, and personality. The warm burgundy and cream color palette creates an inviting, sophisticated atmosphere that feels upscale yet approachable.

## Visual Style

The restaurant template features beautiful, mouth-watering food photography, warm lighting that conveys hospitality, and elegant serif typography. The color scheme uses deep burgundy (#6b2423) paired with warm cream (#f5e6d3) and coral accents, creating a sophisticated yet inviting feel. Every design element emphasizes the dining experience.

## Pages Included

- **Home** — Hero section with stunning restaurant or food imagery, featured dishes, and prominent reservation CTA
- **Menu** — Digital menu organized by course with item descriptions, prices, dietary information, and allergen notes
- **About/Story** — Restaurant history, chef biography, sourcing philosophy, and what makes your restaurant special
- **Reservations** — Online reservation booking system with party size, date/time selection, and special occasion notes
- **Contact** — Address with embedded map, phone number, hours, email, parking information, and walk-in policy

This template emphasizes quality, authenticity, and the welcoming atmosphere that keeps customers coming back.
