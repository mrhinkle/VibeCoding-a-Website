# Consultant Template

**For solo business consultants offering strategy, management consulting, or executive advisory services.**

This template is designed for independent consultants and small consulting firms who want to establish credibility and attract high-value clients. The elegant design with navy and gold colors conveys professionalism and trustworthiness.

## Visual Style

The consultant template features a sophisticated layout with a hero image of a modern office or professional meeting space, serif typography for headings that conveys authority, and clean sans-serif body text for readability. The color palette uses navy blue (#1e3a5f) as the primary color with gold accents (#d4af37) to highlight key information.

## Pages Included

- **Home** — Hero section with your consulting value proposition and prominent CTA to schedule a consultation
- **About** — Your background, experience, credentials, and unique consulting philosophy
- **Services** — Clear breakdown of your core offerings (e.g., strategy development, implementation support, team training)
- **Case Studies** — Detailed case studies showing client challenges, your approach, and quantified results
- **Contact** — Contact form, calendar integration for scheduling, email, and phone number

This template emphasizes trust, expertise, and measurable results to attract clients seeking serious consulting support.
