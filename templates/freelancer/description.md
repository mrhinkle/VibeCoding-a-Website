# Freelancer Template

**For designers, developers, writers, and creative professionals showcasing their portfolio.**

This modern template is perfect for independent creatives looking to land better clients and command higher rates. The sleek charcoal and accent color palette creates a professional yet approachable personal brand.

## Visual Style

The freelancer template features a portfolio-first design with bold typography and plenty of white space to let your work shine. The design uses charcoal gray (#2d3436) as a sophisticated base paired with vibrant coral (#ff7675) and sky blue (#74b9ff) accents for visual interest. Modern, geometric sans-serif fonts convey contemporary expertise.

## Pages Included

- **Home** — Bold hero with your name/brand and best portfolio piece, clear positioning statement, and prominent CTA
- **Portfolio** — Curated selection of your strongest projects with detailed case studies showing brief, challenges, your approach, and results
- **About** — Your story, background, philosophy, core skills and tools, and what makes you different from other freelancers
- **Services** — Clear breakdown of what you offer (e.g., web design, UX/UI, development, copywriting), packages, deliverables, and pricing
- **Contact** — Contact form, email address, location, and calendar link for booking consultation calls

This template positions you as a serious creative professional and makes it easy for potential clients to understand your value and get in touch.
