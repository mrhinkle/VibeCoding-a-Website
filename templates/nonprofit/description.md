# Nonprofit Template

**For community nonprofits, charities, and mission-driven organizations seeking support and volunteers.**

This inspiring template is designed for nonprofits wanting to connect emotionally with donors and volunteers while demonstrating transparency and impact. The fresh green color palette symbolizes growth, hope, and positive change.

## Visual Style

The nonprofit template features authentic photos of community impact, beneficiary testimonials, and volunteer stories. The design uses a vibrant forest green (#2d8659) paired with hopeful light accent colors (#52b788), creating an inviting and trustworthy feel. Friendly, rounded typography conveys accessibility and warmth.

## Pages Included

- **Home** — Inspiring hero highlighting your mission, key statistics, and prominent CTAs for donations and volunteering
- **Mission** — Your organization's story, founding history, core values, and detailed explanation of the cause you address
- **Programs** — Breakdown of each program or initiative, how they serve the community, and measurable impact metrics
- **Get Involved/Donate** — Multiple ways to support: donation processing, volunteer opportunities, sponsorship options, and event participation
- **News** — Blog section featuring impact stories, success stories, beneficiary testimonials, and upcoming events
- **Contact** — Contact form, phone, email, address, social media links, and email newsletter signup

This template emphasizes transparency, community, and the tangible impact that supporters make in your organization's work.
