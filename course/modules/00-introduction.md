**Time: 5 minutes**

## What Is "Vibe Coding"?

Let me be direct: "vibe coding" means building professional websites by describing what you want—your vibe, your vision, your business—and letting AI handle the technical heavy lifting.

You don't write HTML. You don't deploy servers. You don't hire developers. You just tell Claude Code what matters to your business, and it builds something real.

This isn't theoretical. This isn't a template you're trapped in. This is a live website, with your own domain, handling real customer interactions, backed by code you can read and modify.

---

## The Old Way vs. The New Way

### The Old Way (Still Expensive)
**Agencies:** $3,000–$15,000. 4–6 weeks. You describe your site, they build it. Then you're stuck: changes cost money. Scaling is painful.

**Templates (Wix, Squarespace):** $200–$500/year. You're locked into features the template designer imagined. Want something different? You can't.

**DIY Code:** You learn HTML, CSS, JavaScript. It takes months. Your site works but looks amateur. Maintaining it becomes a second job.

### The New Way (What You're Learning)
You + Claude Code + Manus.

**Speed:** Your website from zero to live in under 90 minutes.
**Cost:** Your API costs ($5–$20 depending on complexity) plus optional hosting (~$20/month).
**Control:** Your code, your domain, your rules. Full ownership.
**Maintenance:** Updates take minutes, not meetings.
**Iteration:** Change your mind? Build the new version in an afternoon.

The barrier to entry used to be money or time. It's gone now.

## The Two Tools: Claude Code + Manus

### Claude Code
Claude Code is your builder. It's an AI that understands business, design, and code.

In this course, you'll use Claude Code to:
- Interview you about your vision
- Generate your website
- Wire up forms and email
- Optimize for performance
- Deploy to the live internet

Think of it as a tireless developer who asks exactly the right questions and builds fast.

### Manus
Manus is your designer and maintainer. It's a visual editor that lets you update your website without code.

In this course, you'll use Manus to:
- Generate professional brand images
- Adjust colors, fonts, and layouts
- Update text and images after launch
- Maintain your site over time

Think of it as your in-house web team that you can use whenever you need something changed.

Together, they cover the whole lifecycle: build it, ship it, maintain it. You don't need to hire anyone.

## A Note on Mindset

Building something new is a bit vulnerable. You might wonder:
- What if Claude misunderstands my vision?
- What if the design isn't quite right?
- What if something breaks?

Good news: all of this is fixable. You can iterate. You can refine. Breaking things is part of the process, and we'll show you how to fix them.

This isn't like hiring an agency and waiting weeks. You have instant feedback. You control the direction. If something doesn't feel right, you change it.

Think of this as learning a skill, not just following instructions. It gets easier each time you do it.
